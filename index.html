The Podcast Directory

Welcome. This is the future home of a truly open and shared podcast directory.

Any podcast owner will be able submit their podcast to the directory.

The directory will live on the Ethereum blockchain, a decentralized network of high censorship-resistance.

Features of the directory:

  • The directory will be open and accessible by anyone throughout the world
  • The directory itself will have no paywalls, licenses, or gatekeepers
  • The directory will be collectively owned
  • The owner of a podcast will have the sole ability to add or change that podcast's information in the directory
  • The directory will be a hedge against any encroaching centralization amongst other podcast directories
  • To the maximum extent technically possible, the directory will live indefinitely
